Why Patients Struggle with Traditional Physiotherapy

The real challenges that prevent effective recovery

Monotony

Repeating the same exercises daily drains motivation and makes patients want to quit therapy early.

Low Engagement

Patients disengage because traditional exercises feel like a chore, not a path to recovery.

No Instant Feedback

Patients can't tell if they're doing exercises correctly, leading to improper form and slower progress.

Therapist Dependency

Progress tracking is manual, subjective, and requires constant therapist supervision.
